#### **`.weight-{value}`**  
- **Description:** Sets the font weight of the text to a custom value.  
- **CSS equivalent:**  
  ```css
  font-weight: {value};
  ```  
- **Example usage:**  
  ```html
  <span class="weight-700">
    This text has a font weight of 700.
  </span>
  ```  

    ---
